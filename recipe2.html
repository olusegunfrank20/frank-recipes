Marinated Grilled Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Place the pork along with the rest of the ingredients into a large ziplock bag together.
  2. Prepare the grill for indirect heat and remove the pork from the ziplock and place onto an oiled grill.
  3. Grill for (20 to 25)minutes until the middle of the tenderloin is at 145 degrees.
  4. Add the marinade to a small saucepan, bring to a boil and use as sauce when pork is done cooking
