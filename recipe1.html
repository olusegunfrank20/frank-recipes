Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Fresh Pineapple
  • Honey
  • Jumbo Shrimp

Steps

  1. Whisk together all the marinade ingredients. Add the shrimp and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare grill for medium-high heat.
  3. Remove shrimp from marinade and discard.
  4. Alternate threading shrimp and pineapple onto the skewers, 3 pieces each.
  5. Grill for 4-5 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  6. Serve immediately.
